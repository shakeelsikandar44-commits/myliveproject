1. Save and organize your reports.
2. Track trends by comparing prior results.
3. Confirm fasting/medication instructions before testing.
4. Ask your clinician for clear next steps.
5. Make lifestyle changes where appropriate.
6. Ensure medication adherence when prescribed.
7. Repeat tests when recommended.

**Sources & further reading:** CDC, NIH, LabTestsOnline.
