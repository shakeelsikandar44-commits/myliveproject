A quick reference for the most commonly ordered lab tests and what their values usually mean.

### CBC (Complete Blood Count)
- Hemoglobin, Hematocrit, White blood cells, Platelets

### CMP (Comprehensive Metabolic Panel)
- Glucose, kidney function (BUN/Creatinine), liver enzymes (ALT/AST), electrolytes

### Lipid Panel
- Total cholesterol, LDL, HDL, Triglycerides

### A1C
- Average blood sugar control over months; used to screen and monitor diabetes

**Sources:** CDC, LabTestsOnline, PubMed.
